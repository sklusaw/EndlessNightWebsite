About The Endless Night

The Endless Night is a 5 piece metal/hard rock band formed in Omaha Nebraska during the summer of 2016. The group consists of Guitarist/backup vocals Kyle Hess, vocalist Chad Zephyr,Â Guitarist Ethan Smith, Bassist Johnny Reb and Drummer Scott Klusaw. While the group is fairly new to the Omaha music scene, the members of the band have been playing in the scene with other bands and projects for over a decade.

Members of the band have shared the stage with artists such as Light The Torch, Forever More, Devil You Know, American Head Charge, Motograter, Cage 9, Powerman 5000, The Sammus Theory, Eve to Adam, Adelitas Way and more. The group's influences include All That Remains, Killswitch Engage, Slipknot, Breaking Benjamin, Gemini Syndrome, Mudvayne, Deftones, A Perfect Circle, Red, Incubus, and many other artists both underground and mainstream.

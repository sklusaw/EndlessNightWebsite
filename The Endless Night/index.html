About The Endless Night

The Endless Night is a 5 piece metal/hard rock band formed in Omaha Nebraska during the summer of 2016. The group consists of Guitarist/vocalist Kyle Hess, vocalist Chad Zephyr,Â  Guitarist Ethan Smith, Bassist Johnny "Reb" Boughton and Drummer Scott Klusaw. While the group is fairly new to the Omaha music scene, the members of the band have been playing in the scene with other bands and projects for over a decade.

Members of the band have shared the stage with artists such as Light The Torch, Motograter, Cage 9, The Sammus Theory, Eve to Adam and more. The group's influences include Pantera, All That Remains, Lamb of God, Killswitch Engage, Slipknot, Black Sabbath, Breaking Benjamin, Gemini Syndrome, Mudvayne and many other artists both underground and mainstream.
